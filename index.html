In 2017 omission.studio was engaged to develop the concept, the vision and the design for the Girls Scouts digital experience.

The brief was to develop the product to increase engagement of GenZ girls with the organization as well as to transform the movement into the digital format.

The chosen approach was to include and organize the maximum amount of features into the mobile application. As well as to integrate a fully customizable 3d avatar system and a multi-player game world developed in unity.

Microservice architecture powered by the AWS services was chosen as the backend system. GraphQL was specified as the query language, along with the Front End React Native framework.

High fidelity React Native prototype enabled the team to extensively test the design with the 3 groups of users in New York, Dallas, Washington. User seesions validated the developmental vector, and resulted in the definition of the first release feature set.

In the initial stages of the project my focus was product design, which shifted to front end developement during the prototyping phase.
